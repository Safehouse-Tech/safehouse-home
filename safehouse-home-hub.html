Lora Technology

Monitor environment anywhere

The Safehouse Home Hub monitors the surrounding temperature, humidity, power status and wirelessly transmits the results every 15-minutes to nearby lora gateway.

Alarms are relayed in real-time to the user.
Data is relayed to the cloud, where it can be seen in Viper or exported to other services via APIs.



Up to 8 additional Bluetooth connections

The Safehouse Home Hub is designed to expand the range of sensors, as 8 additional bluetooth sensors like panic buttons, Safefouse-USB and many more can be connected to Home Hub which allows to get different rooms and wider area covered for monitoring and reporting.

Data reporting time from these bluetooth sensors can be customised by the user via Viper.
