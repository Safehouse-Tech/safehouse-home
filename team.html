All you need to know about us

The company behind Safehouse Technology is Secure Sensors Innovative Design Ltd (SSID), registered in 2013. SSID has been involved with many innovative projects since it begun, and has manufactured range of sensors including the unique Safehouse Hub. SSID launched Safehouse Technology in January 2018 as the result of an Innovate UK project that took place in Liverpool.

Our Vision

Within three years, SSID aims to be the organisation of choice in the UK for wireless monitoring services for at-home social care and the maintenance of buildings. Income will come from a monthly service charge for operation and one-off charges for system design, installation and refurbishment. Safehouse will be known as a service that works from the outset and realises benefits for clients with a pragmatic and economic approach. The company is recognised for being customer focused, supportive and results driven.

Lionel Phillips

CEO & Director

Lionel Phillips is the Managing Director of Secure Sensor Innovative Design Ltd with specific responsibility for operations and business development. Lionel has a comprehensive business background in corporate management, with a proven ability to direct in a variety of sectors within multi-national environments. Previously he worked with IBM in New Product Development for banking products and in Public Sector Sales with a £200 million revenue target. As Managing Director of Hyder Business Services (part of Welsh Water) Lionel bought a loss-making division and turned it around to a profit situation in less than a year. With Dimension Data Lionel managed the establishing and launch of two multilingual contact centres; a 400 person site for Apple Inc in Cork and one for Credit Swiss in Switzerland. He has also been the country operations manager for The New Zealand Dairy Board (Anchor) and currently sits on the board of the government 5G test bed for Social Care.

John Griffiths

CTO & Director

John Griffiths is the Technology Director and leads the development of the Safehouse products. He was responsible for the conception and design of the Safehouse system and senor. John is a co-founder and CTO of SSID Ltd. He has set up and successfully run several technology and consultancy companies over the last 30 years, including Griffiths Associates where he is the Managing Director, which implemented the first cloud care support system for Bournemouth Borough Council. John specialises in healthcare technology, Smart City development and data centre creation. He was the ICT Specialist for the Welsh Government for 12 years where he worked with academic institutions and companies such as Orange, Intel, Cisco, BT, Thales, Telefonica and Microsoft. He was the project lead on £70m BT Datacenter (2002), the government representative at the EU Connected Health Think Tank and the ministerial advisor on the Futures and Horizons IT Special Interest Group. Most recently, John was a Technical Specialist in the Department for International Trade for Smart Cities and IoT with responsibility for the Far East.
