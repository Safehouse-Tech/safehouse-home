Keeping you safe in your own home.

Loved ones and those who care for you can stay alert and receive notifications of alarms in an emergency.

The Safehouse App in conjuction with physical, monitoring devices and alarm buttons connects via the internet to a central database and enables those responsible for an individualâ€™s care, to receive notifications and alerts instantly on smart phones and tablets via the Safehouse App.

It can be used by friends and family or professional care workers to add an extra layer of security into the lives of loved ones and those in their care.

The Safehouse App displays data collected from sensors and connected devices, such as the humidity and temperature and location of the service user which can be sent to a care giver via the Google map link.

With the built in messenger service carers and family members can message and call directly through the Safehouse App.

Scheduled visits can be logged and specific daily instructions for an individuals care can also be clearly viewed through the Care Plan.

The App can be used in conjunction with the installed Safehouse devices or along side it without them as a means of contacting individuals during self isolation.
