Safehouse Solutions

Our aim is to improve care for individuals who require support to live independently, and to monitor their environmental conditions such as damp and temperature.

Safehouse can improve the health and well-being of vulnerable people particularly when used in conjunction with public services, care providers, landlords and local communities. As well as benefiting individuals, the service reduces the costs of providing good quality care and well maintained homes.
