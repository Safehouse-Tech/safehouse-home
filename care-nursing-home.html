We can help your staff & managers.

Safehouse Care Home package is an end-to-end solution enabling care home staff to remotely monitor the internal environmental conditions of residents’ rooms at scale, using a low-cost data capture method.

The Safehouse Care Home package comprises of USB sensors, door sensors, personal alarms and high capacity data aggregators as part of the Safehouse App and Viper (SaaS) our Software as a Service.

Safehouse is ideal for care homes who wish to offer additional services to their residents and the families of those under their care. Our care home package has been designed to reduce anxiety resulting from not having regular access to loved ones.

By using Viper (SaaS), all those involved in an individual’s care will be able to view the environmental conditions within resident rooms such as the temperature, humidity, CO2 levels, light levels, noise levels and general air quality of the room. As well as the environmental readings, app users will be able to see how often the patients have left the room or been visited, creating greater awareness about residents’ well-being.

All the equipment arrives preconfigured in a single ‘plug and play’ package, where all the sensors and devices are geospatially tagged, allocated to a room or service user, and labelled ready for installation.

Installation does not require a physical engineer; the devices simply plug into main power sockets, or indeed use strong adhesive pads.

Care home managers and staff alike are be able to remotely monitor the internal conditions of residents rooms and common areas, using the Viper (SaaS) dashboard.

The Safehouse app and also give carers the ability to receive instant notifications from triggered devices, and locate an individual's whereabouts at all times of the day and night and visitors to the care homes.

Panic buttons can be personally worn with a lanyard or clipped onto clothing and run off Bluetooth Low Energy. In addition Panic Buttons can be attached to surfaces around the home in the event of a fall or if the user is not wearing their personal alarm.

Residents' family members can also access the same information within Viper (SaaS) through the Safehouse App. Reducing the number of queries that staff have to action and thus reducing the stress of the family members from not having access to there loved ones.

We can help your family members

Safehouse Care Home package is an end-to-end solution enabling family members to remotely monitor the internal environmental conditions of residents’ rooms, they can also monitor general activity and care plans and communicate more effectively with carers through the messenger service.

The Safehouse Care Home package comprises of USB sensors, door sensors, personal alarms and high capacity data aggregators as part of the Safehouse App and Viper (SaaS) our Software as a Service.

Residents' family members can access the same information as the care homes through the Safehouse App, thus reducing stress of the family members from not having access to their loved ones.

Family members can also voice concerns or simply check up on their loved one via the messenger service, thus creating a simple and effective way of communicating and understanding care plans and clients routines.
