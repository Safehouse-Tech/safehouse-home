Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data Under General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

Secure Sensor Innovative Design Ltd processes data for the following reasons:

  • To help improve the living conditions for people in the premises being monitored
  • To let landlords and other third parties maintain property in good condition
  • To detect if alarm systems require maintenance
  • To allow impaired individuals to live independently for longer, through use of personal alarms and health related sensing and monitoring
  • To assist care providers to improve their care provision
  • To allow relatives and nominated friends of people receiving care to see they are living in good conditions and are being looked after.

The lawful basis for processing personal data as set out in Article 6 of the GDPR is Legitimate Interests.
