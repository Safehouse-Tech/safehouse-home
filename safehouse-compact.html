Lora Technology

At every event

The Safehouse Compact sends a message to the cloud every time it sense an event. In addition to messages triggered by contact, motion or light, it transmits a heartbeat message every 15 minutes with all the rest of the readings to notify the system that it is within range and operational.

Alarms are relayed in real-time to the user.
Data is relayed to the cloud, where it can be seen in Viper or exported to other services via APIs.

Sensor Specifications

Sensor readings are performed at 15-minute intervals.

Communications: LoRa – EU 868-870 MHz, Range – 6-8km, Class A Device
