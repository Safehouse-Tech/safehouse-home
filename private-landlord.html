How can we help private landlords?

Facilitating private registered landlords who are providers of social housing.

Managing secure and comfortable buildings has just got a lot easier. Our building management service is designed to save you time and money and allow you to continue providing well-maintained and managed accommodation for your tenants using the latest affordable technology.

Safehouse can help you to reduce staffing costs and efficiently spot early signs of building decay and antisocial behaviour by installing a non-obtrusive digital service comprising of our low-cost sensors, plug and play infrastructure, Safehouse mobile App and Viper (SaaS) our Software as a Service.

All the equipment arrives preconfigured in a single ‘plug and play’ package, where all the sensors and devices are geospatially tagged, allocated to a room or service user, and labelled ready for installation.

Installation does not require a physical engineer; the devices simply plug into main power sockets, or indeed use strong adhesive pads.

Facilities managers are able to remotely monitor the environmental conditions of buildings reducing compensation payments made out due to badly maintained buildings using the dashboard on the online customer portal.

Safehouse also help landlords to spot early signs of fuel poverty thus reducing impact on social services and NHS & protecting rental income
